## The Offer In Plain English

We help growing B2B service companies turn ambiguous opportunities into defensible scopes before delivery starts.

The pain:

> Projects lose margin because they enter delivery before the problem, scope, risks, assumptions, and success criteria are clear.

The output:

> A Pre-Delivery Decision Map that helps sales, leadership, and delivery decide whether to move forward, resize, pause, reject, or clarify an opportunity before committing team capacity.

## What Counts As Progress

Good signal:

- "This happens to us."
- "I have one right now."
- "Sales and delivery are misaligned."
- "I still have to step in too much."
- "How would this work?"
- "What would it cost?"

Weak signal:

- "Interesting."
- "Cool idea."
- "Looks nice."
- "Maybe later."

## Principle

The goal is not to get compliments.

The goal is to find one buyer with one real ambiguous opportunity who is willing to pay for a clearer pre-delivery decision.
