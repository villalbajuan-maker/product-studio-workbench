# Offer Validation Plan

## Offer

Pre-Delivery Decision Map.

## Unit Of Value

One ambiguous opportunity or project converted into a clearer pre-delivery decision.

## Pilot Scope

Process one real opportunity or project before the team is committed.

## Pilot Output

A Pre-Delivery Decision Map with:

- initial request;
- reframed problem;
- observed signals;
- likely cause;
- value at stake;
- risks if misframed;
- defensible scope;
- proportional next step;
- success criteria;
- sales/delivery handoff.

## Success Criteria

The pilot is promising if the client can show at least three:

- the problem is better formulated;
- scope is clearer and more defensible;
- senior intervention is reduced;
- risks of rework are identified before delivery;
- the team makes a better decision to move forward, pause, reject, resize, or clarify;
- sales and delivery share a clearer handoff;
- the proposed action is more proportional to the value at stake.
