# Pre-Delivery Decision Map

## Commercial Promise

> Turn ambiguous opportunities into defensible scopes before delivery starts.

## Who It Is For

Growing B2B service companies with consultative sales and complex delivery, where founders, partners, or senior leaders still have to interpret opportunities, rescue scopes, or translate between sales and delivery.

## What It Does

It takes one real opportunity or project before the team is committed and turns it into:

- a clearer problem;
- economic context;
- delivery risks;
- defensible scope;
- proportional next step;
- handoff for sales and delivery.

## What It Is Not

- Not a project management tool.
- Not a generic consulting report.
- Not a software build proposal.
- Not an AI transformation offer.
- Not a way to accelerate unclear opportunities into delivery.
