# Artifact Structure

## Pre-Delivery Decision Map

The artifact should be a URL, not a static PDF.

It should demonstrate the result without becoming a full product yet.

## Sections

1. Executive Recommendation
2. Initial Request
3. Reframed Problem
4. Observed Signals
5. Likely Cause
6. Value At Stake
7. Risks If Misframed
8. Defensible Scope
9. Proportional Next Step
10. Success Criteria
11. Sales/Delivery Handoff
12. Guided Questions

## Rule

> The URL must demonstrate the result, not become the product.

## What The Artifact Must Prove

The prospect should understand:

- what decision becomes clearer;
- what ambiguity gets removed;
- how scope becomes more defensible;
- how margin and senior time are protected;
- why delivery should not start yet or what should happen before it does.
