# Executive Brief

## Offer

Pre-Delivery Decision Map.

## Promise

> Turn ambiguous opportunities into defensible scopes before delivery starts.

## Buyer

Growing B2B service companies with consultative sales and complex delivery.

Typical examples:

- agencies;
- consultancies;
- studios;
- implementation firms;
- professional service companies;
- boutique B2B service businesses.

## Pain

These companies often lose margin before delivery begins.

The opportunity enters the pipeline with unclear assumptions, vague scope, solution-shaped requests, or expectations nobody has fully unpacked.

By the time delivery gets involved, the team is already paying for ambiguity through rework, senior dependency, scope drift, and delivery rescue.

## Output

One URL-based decision artifact that clarifies:

- the initial request;
- the reframed problem;
- observed signals;
- likely cause;
- value at stake;
- risks if misframed;
- defensible scope;
- proportional next step;
- success criteria;
- sales/delivery handoff.

## What This Is Not

- Not a generic consulting report.
- Not a project management tool.
- Not a software proposal.
- Not a full product yet.
- Not an AI feature demo.

## Validation Goal

Sell one paid pilot using one real ambiguous opportunity.
