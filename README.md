# Product Studio Workbench

This repository is the working system for a product studio focused on turning ambiguous business problems into defensible products, systems, and ventures.

The studio operates from a simple thesis:

> Organizations lose value when they commit resources to problems that have not been clearly formulated, economically understood, or proportionally scoped.

Our work starts before execution. We convert signals into problems, problems into value, value into priorities, and priorities into proportional action.

## Current Validation Focus

The first commercial validation vehicle is the **Pre-Delivery Decision Map** for growing B2B service companies.

Core promise:

> Turn ambiguous opportunities into defensible scopes before delivery starts.

The validation goal is not to build a full product or brand yet. The goal is to sell one paid pilot using a lean landing page, a sample decision-map URL, and direct outreach to qualified prospects.

## Studio Model

The studio is not a generic agency, software factory, pure consultancy, or SaaS product looking for a problem.

It is a disciplined product studio:

- Paid services fund learning.
- Products capture recurrence.
- Venture partnerships capture upside when a partner brings real external advantage.

We do not accept development as a commodity or equity for poorly formulated ideas. Every build must derive from problem formulation, economic value, and proportional action.

## Current Milestone

**Milestone 1: Commercial Validation Surface**

- Publish a lean landing page.
- Publish a sample Pre-Delivery Decision Map URL.
- Prepare outreach messages.
- List 20 prospects.
- Start 10 conversations.
- Make 3 pilot offers.
- Sell 1 paid pilot.

The landing is the door. The sample artifact is the proof. The paid pilot is the validation.
