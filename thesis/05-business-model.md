# Business Model

## Hybrid Model V0.1

> Operate initially as a disciplined product studio that uses paid services in formulation, architecture, and selective build to generate cash, learn from real problems, and accumulate assets, while identifying patterns that can become owned products or shared ventures.

Partnership is only considered when the partner brings access to market, distribution, domain knowledge, real customers, or a substantial non-technical advantage.

## Capture Principle

> Services fund learning. Products capture recurrence. Partnerships capture upside when there is real external advantage.

## Protection Rule

> Do not accept development as a commodity or equity for poorly formulated ideas. Every build must derive from problem formulation, economic value, and proportional action.

## Strategic Warning

Agency work becomes dangerous when it accepts bad briefs, sells hours, obeys ambiguity, and accumulates no assets.

Service work becomes valuable when it is paid research, disciplined formulation, selective build, and a source of reusable intellectual property.
