# Economic Framing

## Economic Leak V0.1

> The central economic leak is premature or disproportionate commitment of resources.

Resources include:

- leadership attention;
- senior judgment;
- delivery capacity;
- operational hours;
- commercial trust;
- budget;
- margin;
- organizational confidence;
- willingness to change.

## What Organizations Pay Today

Organizations pay for weak conversion between signals and decisions through:

- wasted leadership time;
- operational rework;
- misdirected initiatives;
- uncaptured commercial opportunities;
- margin erosion;
- slow decisions;
- dependency on key people;
- poor data and weak decision quality;
- distrust toward new initiatives;
- strategic opportunity cost.

## Root

> Valuable resources are committed before the problem, value, and proportional response are sufficiently understood.

## Commercial Implication

The sellable value is not abstract clarity. It is reducing the cost of committing capacity, margin, and senior judgment to poorly formulated opportunities or projects.
