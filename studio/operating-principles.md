# Operating Principles

## Current Operating Rule

Sell before building too much.

## Validation Before Infrastructure

Before building internal systems, SOPs, or a full studio operating model, validate that the market wants the first commercial entry point.

## Asset Rule

Every engagement, pilot, or experiment should produce at least one reusable asset:

- insight;
- pattern;
- template;
- case;
- component;
- artifact;
- market signal;
- product opportunity.

## Quality Rule

The studio should be measured by the quality of resource commitment it creates, not the volume of deliverables it produces.
