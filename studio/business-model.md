# Studio Business Model

The studio has three possible value-capture paths.

## 1. Paid Services

Formulation, architecture, and selective build for clients with real problems and budget.

Primary value:

- cash;
- learning;
- cases;
- market access;
- reusable patterns.

Rule:

> No build without formulation.

## 2. Owned Products

Products created from recurring patterns observed across clients, prospects, and market research.

Primary value:

- scalable asset;
- repeatability;
- product revenue;
- intellectual property.

Rule:

> Productize only after recurrence and willingness to pay are visible.

## 3. Venture Partnerships

Shared ventures with partners who bring distribution, real customers, domain knowledge, operational access, or another meaningful non-technical advantage.

Primary value:

- upside;
- domain leverage;
- faster market access.

Rule:

> Do not trade serious work for equity in a poorly formulated idea.
