# Venture Criteria

We do not partner with ideas. We partner with advantage.

## A Venture Partner Must Bring At Least One

- Access to real buyers.
- Existing customers.
- Distribution.
- Deep domain expertise.
- Proprietary data or workflow knowledge.
- Operational access.
- A painful recurring problem they can validate.
- Credibility in a specific market.
- Budget or ability to fund validation.

## Negative Signals

- "I have an idea."
- No customer access.
- No willingness to sell.
- No domain depth.
- No budget.
- No evidence of urgency.
- Wants development in exchange for vague future upside.

## Partnership Rule

> Equity or upside is considered only when the partner reduces market risk in a way we cannot.
