# Studio Positioning

## Internal Positioning

> A disciplined product studio that turns ambiguous business problems into defensible products, systems, and ventures.

## More Precise Version

> A product studio that helps companies formulate the right problem, understand the value at stake, and decide what is worth building before committing resources.

## What We Are Not

- Not a generic agency.
- Not a software factory.
- Not a consultancy that only diagnoses.
- Not a SaaS product looking for a problem.
- Not a venture studio that works for free in exchange for ideas.

## Current Commercial Entry Point

The first commercial validation vehicle is the **Pre-Delivery Decision Map** for growing B2B service companies.
