# Commercial Validation Plan

## Goal

Validate whether growing B2B service companies will pay to clarify an ambiguous opportunity before committing delivery capacity.

## Current Offer

Pre-Delivery Decision Map.

## Validation Target

- 20 prospects listed.
- 10 conversations started.
- 3 concrete pilot offers made.
- 1 paid pilot sold.

## Rule

No asset should delay real conversations by more than 48 hours.

## What Counts As Validation

Not validation:

- likes;
- compliments;
- "this is interesting";
- abstract interest.

Validation:

- a qualified prospect brings a real ambiguous opportunity;
- the prospect connects the pain to margin, senior dependency, rework, or delivery risk;
- the prospect agrees to pay or seriously commits to a pilot.
