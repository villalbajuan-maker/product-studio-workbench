# Start Here

This repository is the working system for **Product Studio Workbench**.

It is designed so a new collaborator can understand the studio thesis, the first commercial validation offer, and the immediate execution path without reading a long conversation thread.

## The Studio In One Sentence

> A disciplined product studio that turns ambiguous business problems into defensible products, systems, and ventures.

## The Core Thesis

Organizations lose value when they commit resources to problems that have not been clearly formulated, economically understood, or proportionally scoped.

Our work starts before execution:

```txt
signals -> problems -> value -> priorities -> proportional action
```

## Current Commercial Experiment

The first validation vehicle is the **Pre-Delivery Decision Map**.

It is for growing B2B service companies that lose margin when ambiguous opportunities move too quickly from sales into delivery.

Commercial promise:

> Turn ambiguous opportunities into defensible scopes before delivery starts.

## What Exists Now

- Studio thesis and business model.
- First offer definition.
- Landing page app generated from the validation prompt.
- Sample Pre-Delivery Decision Map content.
- Outreach messages and validation plan.
- Supabase migration for lead capture.

## What We Are Trying To Validate

We are not trying to validate a full studio, SaaS product, or brand yet.

We are trying to validate this:

> Will a growing B2B service company pay us to process one real ambiguous opportunity before delivery capacity is committed?

## Current Milestone

**Commercial Validation Surface**

- Publish landing page.
- Publish sample decision map.
- List 20 prospects.
- Start 10 conversations.
- Make 3 pilot offers.
- Sell 1 paid pilot.
